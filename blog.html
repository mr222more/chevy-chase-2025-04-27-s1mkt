The Ultimate Guide to Frameless Shower Glass: Design, Installation & Care

April 27, 2025

Frameless shower glass represents a modern luxury upgrade that transforms ordinary bathrooms into sophisticated spaces. Using thick tempered glass panels without metal framing, these installations offer enhanced visual space, superior light transmission, and easier maintenance. Key considerations include glass thickness (3/8 to 1/2 inch), hardware selection, and various treatment options like low-iron or frosted glass. Professional installation is essential, involving precise measurements, factory fabrication, and expert mounting. While requiring a higher initial investment, frameless shower glass typically returns 70-80% of its value in home appreciation while providing decades of reliable service and daily luxury.
