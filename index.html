About Us

Founded in 2010, Chevy Chase Custom Glass began with a vision to revolutionize bathroom design in the DC metropolitan area. What started as a small family business has grown into the region's premier provider of custom shower glass solutions, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Maryland, Virginia, and Washington DC.

Our mission is to transform ordinary bathrooms into extraordinary spaces through innovative design, superior craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service. We believe in the power of quality materials, precise installation, and attention to detail to create spaces that inspire and delight our clients every day.
